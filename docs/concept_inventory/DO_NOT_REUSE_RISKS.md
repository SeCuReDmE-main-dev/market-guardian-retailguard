# Do Not Reuse Risks

Status: risk boundary before scaffold.

## Naming And Public Boundary

- Do not expose reserved private model names in package names, docs, routes, tests, UI text, or white paper artifacts.
- Do not copy private repository paths directly into public-facing docs.
- Do not publish private implementation claims before they have been renamed, repathed, and tested inside this repo.

## Surveillance And Privacy Risks

- Do not implement face recognition or biometric identification in v1.
- Do not persist raw frames by default.
- Do not use confirmed-accusation language.
- Do not keep full customer activity trails when a derived metadata event is enough.
- Do not treat consent signage as a substitute for data minimization.

## AI Hallucination Risks

- Do not let a customer agent add an item without an evidence ID.
- Do not let an agent follow a different person after track purity degrades.
- Do not let an LLM decide basket truth or cash truth.
- Do not convert `unknown` into `inferred` without a traceable event.
- Do not merge two agent states unless the orchestrator records why.

## CodeProject.AI And YOLO Risks

- Do not assume YOLO labels are product identity without catalog/POS corroboration.
- Do not treat object detection confidence as moral intent.
- Do not depend on CodeProject.AI being live for unit tests.
- Do not make GPU availability a default requirement.
- Do not make the CodeProject.AI mesh a source of truth; it is a perception adapter.

## Legacy Stack Risks

- Do not reintroduce MindsDB as a required core dependency.
- Do not make E2B, Datadog, Supabase, PostgreSQL, RethinkDB, or cloud accounts mandatory for the first simulator.
- Do not reuse old production/performance claims unless they are measured in this repo.
- Do not copy broad dependency stacks from older work without proving they are needed.

## Math And Theory Risks

- Do not collapse `dF` into generic falsity.
- Do not overload `D_f` as density.
- Do not present plithogenic or fractal-neutrogeometry language as proven retail-security science before the simulator produces evidence.
- Do not hide simple deterministic rules behind theory words.

## Legal And Compliance Risks

- Do not treat this inventory as legal advice.
- Do not deploy in a real Quebec store without Law 25 review by an accountable privacy role.
- Do not store personal information without a purpose, retention period, and destruction rule.
- Do not skip incident register design.
- Do not omit operator access logs.

## Process Risks

- Do not scaffold before concept inventory exists.
- Do not expand the first build into the whole product.
- Do not skip tests for naming leaks and raw-frame retention.
- Do not publish public claims without a source ledger.
- Do not confuse local feasibility with maintainer or market acceptance.
