## Why SECL

Market Guardian / RetailGuard is a market visual helper for small-store
protection, supervised cybersecurity education, and retail-safety review
training. It is safety-sensitive and abuse-prone if misused, so the project uses
the Secured Educational Cybersecurity License 2.0 instead of a generic software
license.

SECL keeps the intended use visible:

- defensive education;
- fraud-awareness training;
- supervised review;
- small-store protection research;
- human-review-only interpretation.

It does not authorize accusation, enforcement, unsafe surveillance, theft,
fraud, bypass, or criminal automation.
